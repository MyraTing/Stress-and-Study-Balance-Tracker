# Planning

This folder contains the planning documents for the Study Zen, a Stress & Study Balance Tracker project. The project focuses on addressing the growing concern of mental health issues among students, which is caused by academic pressure, deadlines, and heavy workloads. Students are unable to control their study habits, which has a negative impact on their mental health. To address this problem, the proposed project aims to develop a new application, namely StudyZen, which is a Stress and Study Balance Tracker. This application will provide students with the ability to monitor their study habits and stress level, thereby encouraging students to develop healthy study habits. This application will improve students' awareness of their mental health. Moreover, it will provide students with a healthy balance of study and life. This project is important for improving students' health, and it is aligned with the United Nations' third sustainable development goal, which focuses on good health and well-being. The planning phase includes defining project goals, identifying stakeholders, gathering system requirements, and organising tasks through an Agile workflow.
