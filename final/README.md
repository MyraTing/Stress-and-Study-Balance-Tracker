# Study Zen – Stress & Study Balance Tracker

Sustainable Development Goal: SDG 3 – Good Health and Well-Being



## Project Overview

Study Zen is a student wellbeing and productivity tracking system designed to help university students maintain a healthy balance between academic work and mental health.

The system allows students to track their study hours, monitor their stress levels, receive reminders to take breaks, and access relaxation tips and mental health support resources.

This project supports Sustainable Development Goal 3 (Good Health and Well-Being) by encouraging healthier study habits and increasing awareness of student mental wellbeing.



## Project Objectives

The main objectives of the system are:

1. Help students monitor study habits and stress levels
2. Encourage healthy study routines
3. Reduce academic burnout
4. Provide access to mental health support resources
5. Improve student productivity and time management

## Expected Impact

The Study Zen prototype demonstrates how digital tools can support student wellbeing by helping students manage their study routines and monitor their stress levels.

By combining productivity tracking with mental health awareness features, the system promotes a healthier and more balanced academic lifestyle.

This directly supports SDG 3: Good Health and Well-Being.
